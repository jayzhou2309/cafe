Our Story

Coffee is a
conversation,
not a transaction.

Montra started with a single question: what does it feel like to be truly unhurried? We built this space in the heart of Telok Ayer as an answer — a place where the sourcing matters, the extraction is deliberate, and the cup in your hands carries the whole journey from farm to counter.

We work directly with small producers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Taiwan, rotating our offerings with the harvest seasons. No shortcuts. No rush.
